How to Play Hand Cricket

  • Toss: First, call Heads or Tails. If you win, you choose to Bat or Bowl. If the computer wins, it will choose.
  • Batting: Pick a number from 1-6. If the computer's number is different, your number is added to your score.
  • Bowling: Pick a number from 1-6. If the computer's number is different, its number is added to its score.
  • OUT!: If you both pick the same number, the batsman is out, and the innings ends.
  • The Goal: Score more runs than the computer before you get out (or get the computer out)!
