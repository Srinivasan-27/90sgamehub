Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At 90's Game Hub, we have a few fundamental principles that we follow.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized and feature-rich gaming experience, we now require user accounts. The information we collect includes:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect a username, email address, and a password. This is essential for creating and securing your account.
  • Gameplay Data: We save your game progress, high scores, and achievements. This allows for features like cross-device syncing and leaderboards.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with our site, such as which games you play and for how long. This helps us improve our service and recommend games you might enjoy.
  • Browser Cache & Local Storage: We use your browser's storage to temporarily store game assets and save your progress, ensuring a smoother and faster experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and enhance our platform. This includes:

  • Personalizing Your Experience: To save your game states, display your high scores, and provide tailored game recommendations.
  • Community Features: To power leaderboards, friend lists, and other social interactions within the hub.
  • Improving Our Service: Analyzing usage data helps us understand what our players enjoy, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Communication: To send you important updates about your account or our services (you can opt-out of promotional emails).

3. Data Security and Sharing

We are committed to protecting your data. We implement robust security measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate data with partners for analytics and service improvement, but this data will not identify you personally.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information. You can access or delete your account and associated data at any time through your profile settings or by contacting us. Please note that deleting your account is permanent and will erase all your saved game progress and scores.

5. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your site preferences. These are essential for the functioning of your user account. By using our site, you consent to the use of these necessary cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and, if applicable, through an email notification. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us through our Contact Page.
