Word Puzzle Finder

How to Play

  • Find the words from the list in the grid.
  • Words can be hidden horizontally, vertically, or diagonally in any of the 8 directions.
  • Click and drag (or tap and drag) on the grid to select letters and form a word.
  • Found words will be highlighted in the grid and crossed off the list.
  • Use the "Hint" button if you're stuck! It will reveal the first letter of a random unfound word.
