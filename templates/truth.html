How to Play

  • Click SPIN THE WHEEL to start.
  • The wheel will select either TRUTH or DARE and the segment will light up.
  • A random question or challenge will appear below.
  • Complete the task and let the next person play!
  • Use the Reset Game button to reset the wheel and restore the original questions.
  • You can add your own questions and dares for the current game session.
