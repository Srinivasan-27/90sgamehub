Checkers Rules

  • Checkers is played by two players on an 8x8 board.
  • Red pieces move first.
  • Regular pieces can only move diagonally forward.
  • Kings (marked with â™”) can move diagonally in any direction.
  • To capture an opponent's piece, jump over it to an empty square.
  • Multiple captures in one turn are allowed and mandatory.
  • If a piece reaches the opposite side of the board, it becomes a king.
  • The game ends when one player has no pieces left or no valid moves.

How to Play

  • Tap on your piece to select it.
  • Tap on a highlighted square to move.
  • Captures are mandatory - you must take them if available.
  • If you have multiple captures, you must complete them in one turn.
