Combine the tiles to reach the 2048 tile!

How to Play

  • Goal: Combine tiles to reach 2048 or higher!
  • Controls: Use arrow keys or swipe gestures.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.
  • After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no more moves are possible.
  • Pro Tip: Try to keep your highest number in a corner.

On mobile: Swipe in any direction to move tiles.
