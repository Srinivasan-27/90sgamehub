How to Play

Batting:

  • Use ↑ and ↓ or the on-screen buttons to position your batsman.
  • Press Space or tap the "Hit" button to swing the bat.
  • Timing is crucial! Hit the ball as it reaches the batsman for maximum runs.

Fielding:

Fielding is automatic. Your goal is to score more than the target set by the opponent.
